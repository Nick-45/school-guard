Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SchoolGuard™'s functionality, security, and support.

Still have questions? Contact us

How does SchoolGuard™ support both CBE and 8-4-4 curricula?

SchoolGuard™ seamlessly manages both curriculum systems. For CBE, we provide advanced competency tracking, portfolio management, and learning outcome monitoring. For 8-4-4, we offer traditional grading, exam management, and comprehensive report generation—all in one platform.

What CBE features does SchoolGuard™ offer?

Our CBE tools include core competency mapping across 7 universal competencies, digital portfolio management, learning outcome tracking, formative assessment tools, and a dedicated parent portal that explains competency development in simple terms.

How secure is student data on SchoolGuard™?

We implement bank-level security with encrypted data storage, role-based access control, regular security audits, and compliance with international data protection standards. Your school's data is completely secure and private.

What support does SchoolGuard™ offer?

SchoolGuard™ offers 24/7 customer support, training sessions for new users, and regular system updates to ensure optimal performance and security.
